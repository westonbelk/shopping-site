Our Mission

Since 2008 we have been committed to growing the professional gaming scene in North America. We sponsor and equip professional teams such as Team SoloMid, Counter-Logic Gaming, and Team Liquid which compete against international competition in various eSports. Our goal is to make sure that current and future generations of gamers are able to get top quality gaming devices at affordable prices. We work with major companies such as Logitech and Razer to make this dream a reality for all.
